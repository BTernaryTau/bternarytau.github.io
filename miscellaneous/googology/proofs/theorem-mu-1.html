Proof of Theorem μ\muμ-1

By Proposition μ\muμ-2, it cannot be the case that nμ! [<] A(n,n)n\mu! \gs A(n, n)nμ![<]A(n,n). By Proposition μ\muμ-3, it cannot be the case that nμ! [>] A(n,n)n\mu! \gf A(n, n)nμ![>]A(n,n). Therefore, nμ! [=] A(n,n)n\mu! \eqv A(n, n)nμ![=]A(n,n).
